Perchance to Dream At a Glance

The Story:

Perchance to Dream is an exploration of Hamlet told through the music of Radiohead. It takes place in a world much like our own, where algorithms replace human connection and creative expression is commodified.

The story takes place at the Elsinore, a music venue and Chelsea Hotel sort of place, in a world that resembles, but is not, our own. It's set in the future, and explores our relationship to technology and consumerism, how it both isolates and brings us together.

The Concert:

We held our premiere concert on 5/27/23 to a sold out, 400-person show at Pico Union Project in Los Angeles. It was covered by the LA Times, LAist, and CNN correspondent David Daniel.
